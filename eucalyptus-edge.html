Game Overview

A new arena fighter with charm and combat depth.

Eucalyptus Edge blends stylized Australian animal warriors, 3D arena movement, weapon-based combat, ring-out victories, and character-specific fighting styles.

Players build Edge Energy by attacking, defending, dodging, countering, and surviving pressure, then spend it on special abilities that reinforce each fighter's identity.

3D Movement

Fight across full arenas with positioning, spacing, and ring-out pressure.

Weapon Combat

Each fighter uses a distinct weapon style, silhouette, and combat rhythm.

Edge Energy

Build meter through skilled play and unleash powerful character abilities.

Family-Friendly

Stylized action with no blood or graphic violence.

Development Roadmap

From Prototype to Launch

Phase 1

Combat Prototype

Core movement, attacks, blocking, dodging, Edge Energy, UI feedback, local versus flow, and first playable fighters.

Phase 2

Vertical Slice

Polished arena presentation, stronger animation pass, improved combat feedback, and refined match flow.

Phase 3

Multiplayer Integration

Online structure, private lobbies, ranked testing, replay planning, and networked combat validation.

Phase 4

Roster Expansion

Additional fighters, arenas, story content, cosmetics, and character-specific polish.

Phase 5

Launch and Live Support

Steam launch preparation, optimization, supporter rewards, post-launch updates, and future content.
