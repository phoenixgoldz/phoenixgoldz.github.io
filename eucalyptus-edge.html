World of Verdantia

Nature fights back.

Verdantia is a magical wildlife world shaped by eucalyptus forests, crystal caverns, coastal harbors, moonlit rainforests, and festival arenas.

The Edge Festival brings champions together, but The Blight threatens to twist nature and corrupt the fighters sworn to protect it.

Battlefields

Arenas of Verdantia

Arenas are built to feel like memorable landmarks with ring-out pressure, dynamic hazards, and strong environmental identity.

Eucalyptus Summit

The highest point in Verdantia, surrounded by ancient eucalyptus forests and cliffside festival platforms.

Crystal Caverns

A glowing underground arena built around magical crystals, reflective surfaces, and dramatic lighting.

Bamboo Harbor

A coastal harbor village with bamboo platforms, water movement, and interactive arena edges.

Moonlit Rainforest

A night arena filled with glowing flora, dense canopy silhouettes, and magical atmosphere.

Frostpine Ridge

A colder highland arena concept that expands Verdantia’s climate and terrain variety.

Edge Festival Colosseum

A ceremonial competition stage built for champions, crowds, banners, and high-stakes battles.

Development Roadmap

From Prototype to Launch

Phase 1

Combat Prototype

Movement, attacks, blocking, dodging, Edge Energy, local versus flow, and first playable fighters.

Phase 2

Vertical Slice

Polished arena presentation, stronger animation pass, improved match flow, and combat feedback.

Phase 3

Multiplayer Integration

Online structure, private lobbies, ranked testing, replay planning, and networked combat validation.

Phase 4

Roster Expansion

Additional fighters, arenas, story content, cosmetics, and character-specific polish.

Phase 5

Launch and Live Support

Steam launch preparation, optimization, supporter rewards, post-launch updates, and future content.
