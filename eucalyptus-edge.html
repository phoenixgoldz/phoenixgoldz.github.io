Combat Features

Simple to understand. Hard to master.

Every system is designed around clear reads, expressive movement, and skill-based decisions.

3D Arena Movement

Position, sidestep, dodge, pressure opponents toward dangerous edges, and use the full arena space.

Weapon-Based Combat

Each fighter has a signature weapon, attack rhythm, range profile, and combat identity.

Edge Energy

Build energy through attacks, defense, dodges, combos, and pressure, then spend it on special abilities.

Ring-Out Victories

Win by depleting health or forcing opponents out of the arena with smart spacing and pressure.

Blocks and Counters

Defense is active. Timed blocking, counterattacks, and reads turn pressure into opportunity.

No Blood or Gore

Stylized fantasy action with magical impacts, expressive animations, and family-friendly presentation.

World of Verdantia

Nature fights back.

Verdantia is a magical wildlife world inspired by Australian forests, mountains, rivers, coastlines, and ancient animal clans. Every year, champions gather during the Edge Festival to prove their skill through honorable combat.

When The Blight begins twisting nature and amplifying aggression, the festival becomes a fight to protect the balance of the world. Defeating Blighted champions purifies them and restores their connection to the Edge.

The Edge

Verdantia’s Life Force

The natural energy that powers fighter abilities, ultimates, and the heart of the combat system.

The Blight

Corrupted Nature

A spreading force that twists champions, increases aggression, and threatens every clan.

The Festival

A Sacred Competition

What begins as celebration becomes a battle to defend Verdantia from collapse.

FAQ

Common Questions

What is Eucalyptus Edge?

A family-friendly 3D weapon-based arena fighting game starring Australian animal warriors.

What platforms are planned?

PC is the current target platform, with a future Steam release planned.

Is it family-friendly?

Yes. The game uses stylized fantasy combat with no blood or graphic violence.

Will there be local versus?

Yes. Local versus is part of the Phase 1 prototype focus.

Will there be online multiplayer?

Online multiplayer is planned for a later development phase after the core combat loop is proven.

What is Phase 1?

Phase 1 is the playable combat prototype: movement, attacks, defense, Edge Energy, UI, and local versus flow.
