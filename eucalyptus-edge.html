World of Verdantia

A colorful world built for expressive arena combat.

Verdantia is a magical wildlife world inspired by Australian forests, mountains, coastlines, caverns, and festival arenas. Every stage is designed to support movement, spacing, ring-out pressure, and strong visual identity.

The peaceful Edge Festival is interrupted by The Blight, a corruption that twists nature and turns champions against the world they are meant to protect.

Gameplay Identity

Accessible to start. Rewarding to master.

3D Arena Movement

Fight across full arenas with positioning, spacing, sidesteps, dodges, and ring-out pressure.

Weapon-Based Combat

Every fighter uses a distinct weapon, silhouette, rhythm, and combat personality.

Edge Energy

Build meter through skilled play and unleash fighter-specific specials and ultimate abilities.

Family-Friendly Action

Stylized fantasy combat with no blood, gore, or graphic violence.

Prototype Systems

Training tools for testing the combat loop.

The first playable prototype will need training mode tools, test dummies, hit feedback, attack validation, movement testing, and arena flow checks.

Community voting and concept sheets will help decide which training dummy designs best fit the world of Verdantia while still being useful for combat testing.

Development Roadmap

From Prototype to Launch

Phase 1

Combat Prototype

Core movement, attacks, blocking, dodging, Edge Energy, UI feedback, local versus flow, and first playable fighters.

Phase 2

Vertical Slice

Polished arena presentation, stronger animation pass, improved combat feedback, and refined match flow.

Phase 3

Multiplayer Integration

Online structure, private lobbies, ranked testing, replay planning, and networked combat validation.

Phase 4

Roster Expansion

Additional fighters, arenas, story content, cosmetics, and character-specific polish.

Phase 5

Launch and Live Support

Steam launch preparation, optimization, supporter rewards, post-launch updates, and future content.
