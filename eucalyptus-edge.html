Explore Verdantia

A living world of magical battle arenas.

Verdantia is a magical wildlife world shaped by eucalyptus forests, crystal caverns, coastal harbors, moonlit rainforests, icy ridges, and ceremonial battle arenas.

Features

Unite nature. Master the Edge.

Responsive Combat

Tight, readable fighting with dodging, blocking, parrying, attacks, counters, and momentum shifts.

Ring-Out Victory

Battle across 3D arenas with dangerous edges. Knock opponents out to win.

Edge Energy

Build Edge Energy to unleash powerful character abilities and turn the tide.

Magical Worlds

Explore beautiful arenas inspired by Australian forests, caverns, harbors, and festival spaces.

Family Friendly

No blood or graphic violence. Fun, stylized, accessible combat for a wide audience.

Battlefields

Arenas of Verdantia

Arenas are built to feel like memorable landmarks with ring-out pressure, hazards, and strong environmental identity.
