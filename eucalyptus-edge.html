World of Verdantia

A living world shaped by nature and crystal energy.

Verdantia is a magical wildlife world inspired by eucalyptus forests, crystal caverns, coastal harbors, sunbaked red desert outposts, moonlit rainforests, and ancient battle grounds. It is beautiful, dangerous, and built for champions.

Interactive World Map

Welcome to Verdantia

Explore the regions planned for Eucalyptus Edge. Each location is designed as a memorable landmark with its own atmosphere, hazards, and combat identity.

Eucalyptus Summit

Home of the Edge Clans

A high-altitude arena surrounded by ancient eucalyptus trees, wooden platforms, and scenic cliffside views.

Planned focus: ring-out pressure, breakable railings, lantern hazards, and strong vertical atmosphere.
Frostpine Ridge

Where Cold Forges Endurance

A frozen mountain region built around icy ledges, wind pressure, and dangerous arena edges.

Planned focus: slippery surfaces, cold wind pressure, cliff danger, and dramatic snow lighting.
Crystal Caverns

Ancient Power Beneath Verdantia

A glowing underground arena filled with radiant crystals, reflective surfaces, and magical combat lighting.

Planned focus: crystal hazards, underground waterfalls, glowing arena edges, and magical ambience.
Bamboo Harbor

Coastal Village Combat

A vibrant harbor of bamboo platforms, tidal hazards, rope bridges, and shifting battlefield momentum.

Planned focus: moving platforms, tidal pressure, dock edges, and coastal combat flow.
Moonlit Rainforest

Where Ancient Magic Glows

A mystical rainforest arena filled with bioluminescent plants, moonlight, fog, and hidden paths.

Planned focus: atmosphere, glowing plants, low visibility moments, and magical forest energy.
Sunbaked Outback

Land of Endurance and Unyielding Spirit

A windswept desert arena built around ancient carvings, red rock cliffs, totem banners, and blazing sunlight.

Planned focus: sand gust hazards, open sightlines, ring-out pressure, red dune atmosphere, and endurance-based arena design.
Blightroot Hollow

The Corrupted Heart of Verdantia

A once-sacred grove where ancient roots twist and pulse with dark energy — living roots, toxic pools, and spreading corruption under perpetual twilight.

Planned focus: root eruption hazards, corruption pools that shrink safe space, collapsing root bridges, and multi-level ring-out combat.
Edge Festival Colosseum

The Heart of the Championship

The ceremonial grounds where champions gather to prove their skill, honor their clans, and protect Verdantia.

Planned focus: championship spectacle, local versus presentation, crowd energy, and final arena polish.
Training Grounds

Every fighter enters the arena with a role.

Fighters are built around signature weapons, movement styles, readable silhouettes, and clear combat identities. Phase 1 focuses on proving the core loop through local versus gameplay and training arena combat.

The Blight

A corruption spreading through Verdantia.

The Blight twists nature, amplifies aggression, and threatens every clan. What begins as a sacred combat festival becomes a fight to protect the balance of the world.

Story Direction

The Edge must be restored.

Champions who fall to The Blight can be purified through combat. Victory is not only about defeating opponents, but restoring the natural life force that connects Verdantia.

Game Features

Unite Nature. Master the Edge.

Responsive Combat

Tight and readable action with light attacks, heavy attacks, blocking, dodging, counters, and combo chains.

Ring-Out Victories

Every arena edge matters. Knock opponents out of the battlefield or win through health pressure.

Edge Energy

Build Verdantia's natural life force through active play and spend it on special and ultimate abilities.

Family-Friendly Fantasy

Stylized effects, expressive animation, magical impact, and no blood or graphic violence.
