Studio Identity

A new studio built for original game worlds.

PhoenixGold Game Studios is evolving from a student portfolio into a studio-first brand focused on commercial game development, public-facing releases, and long-term original IP.

The studio is focused on polished presentation, memorable characters, expressive gameplay, and player-first worlds that can grow beyond a single release.

Our first published game will be Eucalyptus Edge, a family-friendly 3D weapon-based arena fighter planned for PC and Steam.
