What Is It?

A smaller game that keeps Verdantia alive.

MegaBlast Mayhem is designed as a companion release before the larger Eucalyptus Edge arena fighter. It introduces players to the characters, colors, powers, and playful side of Verdantia through a faster, simpler arcade format.

It is not replacing the main game. It is a way to build momentum, share the world earlier, and give players something fun to play while the Unreal Engine 5.8 prototype continues development.

Design Goal

Simple to start. Fun to replay.

Easy movement, readable bombs, quick power-up decisions, and character personality in every sprite.
