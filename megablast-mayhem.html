Gameplay

Easy to learn. Chaotic to master.

Plant bombs, destroy crates, collect upgrades, dodge chain reactions, outsmart your opponents, and survive until the final round.

Versus Mode

Battle with up to four local players, fill empty seats with bots, and select from four bot difficulty levels.

Region Tour

Journey through all five regions, earn medals, continue saved tours, and reach the festival finale.

Sonia Finale

Complete the Region Tour and face Sonia in the championship battle that concludes the festival.

Random Arena

Generate new region-themed battleground layouts for unpredictable and highly replayable matches.

Chaos Bombs

Gravity, Chain, MegaBlast, and Ghost mutations combine with powerups and match modifiers.

Eight Fighters

Play as Koda, Ripper, Kiri, Banjo, Atlas, Echo, Wren, and unlockable finale challenger Sonia.

55 Achievements

Complete story, combat, powerup, exploration, bot, challenge, and secret achievements.

Controller Support

Navigate menus and compete using keyboard controls or SDL2-compatible game controllers.

Display Options

Configure fullscreen, windowed mode, resolution, audio, visual presentation, and other settings.

Playable Heroes

Meet the MegaBlast Festival competitors

Eight fighters from across Verdantia enter the annual tournament, each bringing their own personality, history, and unmistakable fighting spirit.

Koda

A courageous koala guardian from Eucalyptus Summit who fights to protect his home and friends.

Banjo

A clever sugar glider rogue whose speed and confidence make him difficult to corner.

Kiri

A bold kookaburra competitor who brings bright energy and fearless determination to every arena.

Ripper

A powerful Tasmanian devil brawler who thrives in the center of explosive chaos.

Atlas

A proud emu warrior whose reach, precision, and commanding presence make him a formidable rival.

Echo

A resourceful platypus fighter shaped by the frozen beauty and danger of Frostpine Ridge.

Wren

A fearless kangaroo boxer whose explosive strength makes him one of Verdantia’s toughest competitors.

Sonia

The mysterious white tigress champion awaiting anyone strong enough to reach the festival finale.

Tour the battlegrounds of Verdantia

Every region contains two handcrafted arenas, a distinct visual identity, and its own place in the annual MegaBlast Festival.

Eucalyptus Summit

Moss-covered ruins, towering roots, waterfalls, and elevated forest paths.

Crystal Caverns

Bamboo Harbor

Frozen stone, snow-covered paths, mountain pines, glittering ice, and biting winds.

Project History

From classroom prototype to commercial release

MegaBlast Mayhem began as a university project approximately four years before its commercial release. The original concept was created by Trevor Hicks and co-developed with classmate Calvin Bryant during an introductory JavaScript and Object-Oriented Programming course.

What began as a classroom prototype evolved into PhoenixGold Game Studios’ first commercial game and the first playable title set in the world of Verdantia.

Its story concludes with Sonia’s challenge, celebrates the tournament, and ends with one promise: “The adventure continues in Eucalyptus Edge.”
