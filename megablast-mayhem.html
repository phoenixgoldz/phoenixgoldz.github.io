Gameplay

Local multiplayer festival battles

MegaBlast Mayhem keeps the action simple to learn and chaotic to master: plant bombs, break crates, collect powerups, dodge explosions, and survive until the final round.

Versus Mode

Battle with up to four local players, fill empty seats with bots, and choose from four difficulty levels.

Region Tour

Travel through all five regions, earn medals, continue saved tours, and face Sonia in the festival finale.

Random Arena

Generate fresh battleground layouts themed around each Verdantia region for endlessly varied matches.

Chaos Bombs

Gravity, Chain, MegaBlast, and Ghost mutations combine with powerups and match mutators.

Eight Fighters

Play as Koda, Ripper, Kiri, Banjo, Atlas, Echo, Wren, and unlockable finale challenger Sonia.

55 Achievements

Complete story, combat, powerup, exploration, bot, and secret achievement challenges.

Controller Ready

Full keyboard and controller navigation with Xbox, PlayStation, Nintendo, and DirectInput support.

Built for Every Screen

Resolution and display options support handhelds, widescreen, ultrawide, 4:3, and multi-monitor setups.

Playable Heroes

The Eucalyptus Edge cast enters MegaBlast

Inspired by the classic bomb arena games that defined couch multiplayer, MegaBlast Mayhem reimagines the genre using the heroes, locations, and lore of the Eucalyptus Edge universe.

Koda

Guardian of Eucalyptus Summit.

Banjo

Sugar glider rogue built for quick movement.

Kiri

Kookaburra warrior with bright blue flair.

Ripper

Tasmanian devil brawler made for chaos.

Atlas

Emu spear fighter with reach and precision.

Echo

Platypus fighter wielding icy crystal tonfa.

Wren

Kangaroo boxing brawler with explosive power.

Sonia

White tigress warrior and future Verdantia legend.

Five Regions · Ten Arenas

Tour the battlegrounds of Verdantia

Every region includes two handcrafted arenas, a distinct visual identity, and its own place in the MegaBlast Festival.

Eucalyptus Summit

Mossy ruins, great roots, waterfalls, and elevated forest paths.

Crystal Caverns

Glowing crystals, ancient stone, lantern light, and dangerous underground passages.

Bamboo Harbor

Busy docks, bamboo walkways, rope posts, lanterns, and open water.

Frostpine Ridge

Frozen stone, snow-covered paths, mountain pines, and biting winds.

Sunbaked Outback

Red earth, weathered sandstone, dry grass, and ancient desert formations.

The Verdantia Journey

The first complete chapter of Verdantia

MegaBlast Mayhem began as a university project and grew into PhoenixGold Game Studios’ first commercial game: a polished, self-contained MegaBlast Festival adventure set before Eucalyptus Edge.

Its story ends with Sonia’s challenge, celebrates the tournament, and closes with one promise: “The adventure continues in Eucalyptus Edge.”
