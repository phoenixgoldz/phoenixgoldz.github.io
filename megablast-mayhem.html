What Is It?

A smaller game that keeps Verdantia active.

Eucalyptus Edge is still the flagship 3D arena fighter. MegaBlast Mayhem is a smaller companion game designed to introduce the characters and world in a faster, simpler, arcade format.

This gives players something connected to Verdantia while the larger Unreal Engine 5.8 prototype continues development.

Design Goal

Simple to start. Fun to replay.

Clear movement, readable bombs, quick power-up decisions, and recognizable character personalities.
