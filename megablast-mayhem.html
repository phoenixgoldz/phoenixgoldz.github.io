A Verdantia spin-off

The first playable step into Verdantia

Eucalyptus Edge: MegaBlast Mayhem is designed as the first released game in the Eucalyptus Edge franchise. It gives players a fun, replayable way to meet the cast and explore Verdantia while the larger 3D weapon-based arena fighter continues development in Unreal Engine 5.8.

Gameplay

Bombs, powerups, chaos, and local multiplayer

1–4 Local Players

Play solo against bots or battle with friends on one machine.

Adaptive Bots

Empty seats are filled by bots that dodge, chase, plant bombs, and improve over time.

Chaos Bombs

Special bomb mutations create unpredictable matches with explosive surprises.

First-to-3 Matches

Win rounds, survive the arena, and become the final fighter standing.
