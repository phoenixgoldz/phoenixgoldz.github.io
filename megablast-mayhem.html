Mossy stone, roots, waterfalls, rope bridges, and forest platforms.

Crystal Caverns

Snow tiles, frozen stone, icy paths, pine trees, and mountain winds.

Bamboo Harbor

Dock planks, bamboo floors, rope posts, crates, lanterns, and ocean edges.

Red dirt, sandstone, dry grass, desert shrubs, ancient rocks, and dust.

Bioluminescent plants, fog, moonlight, hidden paths, and magical foliage.

Why it exists

A playable bridge to the main game

Eucalyptus Edge: MegaBlast Mayhem releases first to build the community, introduce the cast, and give players a fun way to spend time in Verdantia while the larger Unreal Engine 5.8 arena fighter continues development.

The goal is simple: let players recognize Koda, Banjo, Kiri, Ripper, Atlas, Echo, Wren, Sonia, and the regions of Verdantia before they arrive in the main Eucalyptus Edge experience.
