Playable Heroes

The Eucalyptus Edge cast enters MegaBlast

Eucalyptus Edge: MegaBlast Mayhem is a PhoenixGold Game Studios original based on the Super Bomberman on the SNES made in 1992.

This is our own version of that classic Super Bomberman that we loved to play as kids!

We hope you enjoy our take on this retro style!

Verdantia Arenas

Festival battlegrounds from the world map

Each arena is designed to feel like a pixel-art version of the regions shown on the main Eucalyptus Edge page.

Eucalyptus Summit

Mossy stone, roots, waterfalls, rope bridges, and forest platforms.

Crystal Caverns

Blue-purple crystal floors, underground stone, lanterns, and glowing cave edges.

Frostpine Ridge

Snow tiles, frozen stone, icy paths, pine trees, and mountain winds.

Bamboo Harbor

Dock planks, bamboo floors, rope posts, crates, lanterns, and ocean edges.

Sunbaked Outback

Red dirt, sandstone, dry grass, desert shrubs, ancient rocks, and dust.

Moonlit Rainforest

Bioluminescent plants, fog, moonlight, hidden paths, and magical foliage.
